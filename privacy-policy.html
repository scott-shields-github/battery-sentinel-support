Battery Sentinel does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is completely protected because we don't access any of your information.

Information We Collect

None. Battery Sentinel does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics. The app operates entirely on your device without any data collection mechanisms.

Information We Store

Battery Sentinel stores only your app preferences (such as warning thresholds and notification settings) locally on your Mac using standard macOS preferences storage. This information:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is not transmitted anywhere
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties

Information We Share

None. Since we don't collect any information, we have nothing to share with third parties.

Network Access

Battery Sentinel does not require or use any network connections. The app operates entirely offline and does not communicate with any external servers.

Third-Party Services

Battery Sentinel does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Data Security

Your app preferences are stored using macOS's secure preferences system and are protected by your Mac's built-in security features. Since no data is transmitted, there are no network-related security concerns.

Children's Privacy

Battery Sentinel is safe for users of all ages as it does not collect any personal information from anyone, regardless of age.

System Information Access

Battery Sentinel accesses only the following system information to provide its core functionality:

  • Battery level and charging status - To display current battery information and determine when to show warnings
  • System power management - To detect sleep/wake cycles and charging state changes

This information is used only for app functionality and is not stored, logged, or transmitted.

App Store and Apple

When you download Battery Sentinel from the Mac App Store, Apple may collect information about your download according to their own privacy policy. We do not have access to this information.
