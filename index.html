‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Battery Sentinel doesn't appear in my menu bar

Solution:

  1. Make sure Battery Sentinel is running (check Activity Monitor)
  2. Try quitting and restarting the app
  3. Check if the menu bar icon is hidden - try expanding the menu bar
  4. Restart your Mac if the issue persists

How do I customize warning thresholds?

Steps:

  1. Click the Battery Sentinel icon in your menu bar
  2. Select "Preferences..." from the menu
  3. Adjust the sliders for Low Battery and Critical Battery warnings
  4. Click "Save Settings" to apply changes

Battery Sentinel doesn't start at login

Solution:

  1. Click the Battery Sentinel menu bar icon
  2. Make sure "Launch at Login" is checked
  3. If still not working, manually add to Login Items:
  4. System Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Login Items ‚Üí Add Battery Sentinel

How do I test if warnings are working?

Testing:

  1. Click the Battery Sentinel menu bar icon
  2. Select "Test Low Warning" or "Test Critical Warning"
  3. A preview alert will appear showing how warnings look
  4. Use this to adjust your preferences

Can I disable certain types of warnings?

Yes! In Preferences:

  • Toggle off "Enable low battery warnings" to disable low alerts
  • Toggle off "Enable critical battery warnings" to disable critical alerts
  • You can customize each type independently

Why don't I get warnings while charging?

This is by design! Battery Sentinel automatically detects when your Mac is plugged in and disables warnings since your battery is charging. Warnings resume when you unplug.

How do I uninstall Battery Sentinel?

Steps:

  1. Quit Battery Sentinel from the menu bar
  2. Open Applications folder
  3. Drag Battery Sentinel to Trash
  4. Remove from Login Items if needed (System Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Login Items)

üêõ Troubleshooting

Battery Sentinel uses too much CPU

Battery Sentinel is designed to be lightweight. If you notice high CPU usage:

  • Restart the app
  • Check for macOS updates
  • Contact support if the issue persists

Warnings appear too frequently

Adjust your settings:

  • Increase the time between warnings in Preferences
  • Raise the warning thresholds
  • Battery Sentinel has built-in cooldown periods to prevent spam
