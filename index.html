üß™ Testing your warning settings

Preview your alerts:

  1. Click the Battery Sentinel menu bar icon
  2. Choose Test Low Warning or Test Critical Warning

  4. Perfect for adjusting preferences before real alerts

üîï Disabling specific warning types

Customize your experience:

  • Open Preferences from the menu bar
  • Toggle off "Enable low battery warnings" to disable gentle alerts
  • Toggle off "Enable critical battery warnings" to disable urgent alerts
  • Each type can be customized independently

üîå Why no warnings while charging?

This is smart design! Battery Sentinel automatically detects when your Mac is plugged in and pauses warnings since your battery is charging. Alerts resume when you unplug.
